Gluten Free Pretzel


How to Make Chewy Gluten-Free Soft Pretzels

Soft pretzels are just about perfect. They’re chewy, salty and great with everything from a beer to a salad. The thing is: they’re chewy. And gluten-free flour doesn’t do chewy well.

Ingrediants

  • 2 cups finely ground white rice flour
  • 1 cup brown rice flour
  • 3/4 cup tapioca starch
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon xanthan gum
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 cup warm water plus 2 tablespoons
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 tablespoons light corn syrup
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Water Bath

  • 16 cups water
  • 1/4 cup baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar

Toppings

  • Kosher Salt
  • (Optional)Cheese
  • (Optional)Pepperoni
