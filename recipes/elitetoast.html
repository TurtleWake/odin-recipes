Elite Toast

Are you ready to have the best toast of your life?

Elite Toast will change your perspective on how you view toast and could possibly ruin all other toast for you in the future, so proceed with caution!

Ingrediants

  • Bread of your choice
  • Topping of your choice(Avocado or Pure Nut Butters are chef's kiss)

Instructions

Toaster Oven

  1. Turn the timer as high as it will go
  2. Put the toast into the toaster oven
  3. Go watch an episode of a tv show or anime
  4. Once you start to smell smoke go check the bread, if you see any color other than black then it needs more time
  5. Once completely black take it out and enjoy
